Favorite Food

Ramen

I love ramen! It's my all-time favorite dish, not just because of the rich, savory broth, but also because of the ideal blend of noodles, meat, and toppings that make for a cozy and gratifying dinner. Ramen, whether in a conventional soy-based broth or a spicy miso variant, never fails to satisfy. The mix of flavors and textures in each bowl makes every bite intriguing, and it's the ideal dish for every occasion. For me, nothing beats the comfort and satisfaction that a steaming bowl of ramen provides.

Hotpot

I personally enjoy hotpot, and it has a special place in my heart. Whenever we conduct ancestral worship at home, my mother makes a hotpot to commemorate our ancestors. For me, hotpot is more than just a dinner; it's an opportunity for family togetherness. We gather around the table, laughing and telling stories about our days while eating a delicious feast. The beauty of hotpot is that it combines so many various ingredients in one pot, allowing everyone to tailor their meal to their preferences. It's a great way for me to reconnect with my family through a shared culinary experience, remembering about home-cooked meals and making new memories together. Hotpot symbolizes warmth, camaraderie, and the satisfaction of sharing delicious cuisine with loved ones.

DIMSUM

I just adore dim sum! Whenever I return home, I look forward to spending the morning with my grandmother.This outing is noteworthy because it allows us to connect over tasty dumplings and buns. While we eat, my grandma tells us about her life and the difficulties she had when she was younger.These chats strengthen our bond and make the dinner even more memorable. Dim sum is more than simply food to me; it's a cherished tradition that fosters long-lasting memories with my family.
